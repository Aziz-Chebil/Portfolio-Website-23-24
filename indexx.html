Model UN

Model UN constitute a big part of my life. I did my first MUN conference in the pandemic, I remember how shy and hesitant I was. Yet, the idea of representing a country and speaking on behalf of a whole nation somehow impressioned me. Thus, I attended more and more MUN conferences online during the pandemic. Each conference brought new people, new ideas, and new perspectives for me. The more I participated in simulations, the more I became self-confident in my speeches, the more I learned how to convince my fellow delegates, the better I wrote positions and resolutions papers and the more awards I started to win.
Simply, MUN makes me happy.
